Privacy Policy

Your Data is Secure with Us.

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

OmniServe collects personal data necessary to provide our services. This includes your name, email address, payment information, and any communication held between you and service providers on our platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly to facilitate bookings, process payments, and improve user experience. Medical or health-related data shared during virtual consultations (such as dental pre-screenings) is end-to-end encrypted and never sold to third parties.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure, or destruction of data. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

4. Third-Party Sharing

We only share data with verified service providers that you explicitly book through our platform, and with payment gateways necessary to process your transactions.
